## General Interview Tips for This Role

1. **Use the STAR method** (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for all behavioral questions.
2. **Research LSEG** — Understand their products, recent news, and strategic direction. Mention specifics.
3. **Prepare code samples** — Be ready to write Python/Java/Shell scripts on a whiteboard or shared screen.
4. **Quantify your achievements** — "Reduced regression time by 70%" is stronger than "Improved regression testing."
5. **Show domain interest** — Even if you haven't worked in financial markets, demonstrate that you've done your homework.
6. **Ask interviewer-tailored questions** — Prepare different questions for each interviewer. For Amy (Director): ask about AI-driven QA strategy, quality metrics, career progression. For Andy (QE Manager, likely direct manager): ask about day-to-day team work, tech stack, first 90 days. See Q76 for specific prepared questions.
7. **Align with values** — Weave Integrity, Partnership, Excellence, and Change into your answers naturally.
8. **Be honest about gaps** — If you lack experience in an area (e.g., FIX protocol), say so, then explain how you'd ramp up quickly.

## How to Use This Guide

- **Review each category** in order, or focus on areas where you feel less confident.
- **Practice out loud** — Speaking your answers is very different from reading them.
- **Personalize** — Replace generic examples with your own real experiences.
- **Time yourself** — Aim for 2–3 minute answers for most questions.
- **Study the job description** — Many questions are directly tied to JD requirements. Keep the JD handy while preparing.
