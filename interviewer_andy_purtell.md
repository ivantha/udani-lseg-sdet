4. **Integration testing** — His Elektron Integration Support background means he likely values strong integration and system testing skills. He may ask about testing approaches for distributed systems, API integrations, message queues, or data pipelines.

5. **Financial data domain** — Andy has spent his career in financial data delivery. He may assess whether the candidate understands (or can quickly learn) financial market concepts — market data feeds, trading systems, order management, or regulatory requirements.

6. **Team fit and growth potential** — As the likely direct manager, he'll be evaluating whether the candidate would be a good fit for his team culture, whether they can work independently (as the JD states), and whether they show potential for growth into more senior roles.
