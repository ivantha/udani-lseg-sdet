## How to Use This Package

### Recommended preparation order

1. **Start with the strategy** — Read `interview_guide_udani.md` first. It covers the two interviewers, how to handle the career gap, and the overall narrative to present.
2. **Know the interviewers** — Review `interviewer_amy_lee.md` and `interviewer_andy_purtell.md` to understand who you're speaking with.
3. **Prioritise weak areas** — The interview guide and CV critique identify specific gaps. Focus preparation time on:
   - Career gap narrative (`10_personal_and_career.md`, Q71–Q72, Q75)
   - Financial domain bridging (`05_financial_domain.md`)
   - Performance testing honest framing (`03_performance_testing.md`)
   - AI & modern QA for Amy Lee (`11_ai_and_modern_qa.md`)
4. **Work through the question bank** — Practice answers out loud, using the STAR method for behavioural questions. Aim for 2–3 minutes per answer.
5. **Prepare your questions** — See Q76 for interviewer-tailored questions to ask.

### Key themes across all materials

- **Bridge, don't bluff** — For gap areas (financial domain, performance testing, CMMI), use the pattern: *"Here's what I know → Here's what connects → Here's how I'll close the gap."*
- **Tailor to the interviewer** — Amy wants strategic outcomes; Andy wants hands-on specifics. See Q77 for detailed guidance.
- **Quantify everything** — "Reduced regression from 5 days to 2 days" beats "Improved regression testing."
- **Own the career gap** — Frame it as deliberate investment (MSc + ISTQB Advanced), not absence.
